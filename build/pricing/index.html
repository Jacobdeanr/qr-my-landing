What's included on every plan

Free and Pro share the full toolkit. The same customization, the same analytics, the same QR types. The only difference is how many codes and scans you get.

  • Seven QR types — URL, Text, vCard, WiFi, Phone, SMS, Email. Static or dynamic where it makes sense.
  • Dynamic destinations on URL codes — change where a printed code points whenever you want. The printed image stays the same.
  • Module shapes and color — square, rounded, circle, or gapped modules. Six on-brand colors.
  • Live preview while you build, so you see exactly what you'll print.
  • Per-scan analytics — device, OS, country, city when available, time. A 7-day chart and a top-locations breakdown.
  • Scan history filtering by code, device, or date range.
  • PNG download at print resolution.

What's the difference between Free and Pro

Two numbers. Five codes versus fifty. Ten thousand scans per month versus one hundred thousand. Pro is the answer when you have more campaigns running at once or one campaign producing a lot of traffic — not a different feature set.

Billing

Pro is $10 per month, billed by Stripe, renewing monthly until you cancel. You can cancel anytime from the Stripe customer portal — cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period and your codes go back to Free's limits afterward. We don't refund partial months.

Need more than fifty codes

Email hello@qrmy.app. We respond within one business day. Tell us roughly how many codes and scans, and what kind of campaign — we can usually get you running on a custom plan the same week.
