About

A small tool for printable, editable QR codes.

QR My is built for the people who actually print QR codes — not for procurement teams.

You make a code, print it on a menu or a flyer or a window decal, and keep the destination editable. If the brunch menu becomes the Mother's Day menu, you swap the destination — the printed code stays the same. Every scan records device, OS, country, city when available, and time.

Why this exists

Most QR tools fall in two camps. Free generators don't track anything and won't let you edit. Enterprise platforms hide pricing, gate demos, and want a sales call before you can print one code. Both miss the people who actually print QR codes — the marketing manager at a three-location restaurant, the event organizer printing two hundred badges, the retailer with a sale flyer due Friday.

We built QR My for that audience. Self-serve. Pricing on the page. Five codes free, no credit card. The same customization toolkit on every plan.

What you get

  • Edit the destination after the code is printed.
  • Per-scan analytics — device, OS, country, city when available, time.
  • Seven QR types — URL, Text, vCard, WiFi, Phone, SMS, Email.
  • Module shapes and six on-brand colors. Same toolkit on every plan.
  • A live builder you can try before signing up.

What we won't do

  • We won't email you weekly.
  • We won't gate basic features behind a paywall.
  • We won't ask for a credit card to start.
  • We won't track the people who scan your codes — only that scans happened.
